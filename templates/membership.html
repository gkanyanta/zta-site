Membership

By becoming a member of the Zambia Tennis Association you help support the growth of tennis in our country. Members receive updates on events, eligibility to enter national tournaments, access to coaching resources and the chance to influence the future of the sport through participation in our general meetings.

Membership Benefits

  • Entry into ZTA sanctioned tournaments and events
  • Access to coaching clinics and training resources
  • Discounts on equipment and apparel from partner stores
  • Eligibility for national team selection
  • Voting rights at annual general meetings
