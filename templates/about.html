About the Zambia Tennis Association

The Zambia Tennis Association (ZTA) is the national governing body for tennis in Zambia. We are dedicated to promoting and developing the sport of tennis at all levels, from grassroots programmes for children to competitive opportunities for elite athletes. As a member federation of the International Tennis Federation (ITF), we align our activities with global standards and strive to represent Zambia on the international stage.

Our Mission

To nurture and inspire a love of tennis across Zambia by providing accessible programmes, quality coaching and competitive opportunities for players of all ages and abilities.

Governance & Board

The association is governed by an elected executive committee. The board oversees strategic direction, ensures compliance with ITF regulations, and manages partnerships and development projects. Board members include representatives from across the regions of Zambia to ensure diverse representation.

Affiliations & Partnerships

We are proud to be affiliated with the International Tennis Federation and the Confederation of African Tennis. Through strategic partnerships with schools, clubs and sponsors, we deliver programmes that reach communities nationwide.

Our Values

Inclusivity

We believe tennis should be accessible to everyone, regardless of age, gender or background. Our programmes aim to break down barriers and make tennis a sport for all.

Excellence

We strive for excellence on and off the court. From coaching standards to player development, we are committed to achieving the highest levels of performance.

Integrity

We uphold the values of fair play, transparency and accountability in all our activities. Our governance practices ensure trust among stakeholders.
